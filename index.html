MOVEO

Who we are?

MOVEO is a university startup dedicated to connecting workers who need reliable transportation with people willing to lend their vehicles safely and affordably.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to democratize access to vehicles through a fair and flexible lending model, promoting mobility as a right and an opportunity for thousands of workers.

Subscriptions

At Moveo, we work with a vehicle subscription model, where users pay for the time they use the car they choose, in a flexible and affordable way.
